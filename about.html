About

RettLife.ca is a Canadian hub
for individuals living with Rett syndrome and those who care for them.

This is a place to access and share Rett syndrome experiences and resources
This site is sponsored by Acadia Pharmaceuticals Inc. We advance breakthroughs in neurological conditions, with a focus on areas where no approved therapies currently exist.
Acadia is headquartered in San Diego, California, with offices in Princeton, New Jersey and Toronto, Ontario, Canada and ambitions to serve patients globally.
cadia expanded to Canada in 2023 and are proud to have joined an esteemed and advanced medical landscape and a dedicated and savvy Rett syndrome community in Canada
We will continue to work towards our commitments to grow meaningful awareness of Rett syndrome and ease the debilitating symptoms experienced by Rett syndrome patients and their families living in Canada.
Sign up to be notified when new resources and new stories from families living with Rett syndrome become available on RettLife.ca!
This website is intended for use by Canadian residents.
